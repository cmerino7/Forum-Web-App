What is ACME Forums?

ACME Formus is an online forum dedicated to being a place for quizative minds from all over the world to gather and partake in stimulating intellectual discussions!

We hope our users come to make and find ACME Forums a place to respectfully share their voices with the world, to look for the voices of others, whether they are one's views on a topic, or even just seeking an answer as help and to learn from others.

All kinds of topics are welcome on this forum as we wish to encourage diverse opinions and knowledge to be shared with the world. The internet, after all, is supposed to be the accumulation of all human knowledge.

So what are you waiting for? Put on your thinking cap and dive into the ocean of knowledge that is ACME Forums today!

USER TERMS OF SERVICE

Here at ACME Forums, we only ask two general and simple rules of our users:

BE RESPECTFUL

As we encourage free thinking, we are aware that many of our users' views will clash with one another. Disagreement is the catalyst for deep and heated debates and discussions! So it's perfectly normal and acceptable, MOST of us are only human, after all. We at ACME Forums simply ask that users keep their debates and disagreements civil.

NO MALICOUS SPREADING OF MISINFORMATION

Ignorance is the death of progress! While it's perfectly fine to be incorrect on a factual issue, ACME Forums will not tolerate intentional attempts to mislead other users with misinformation, slander, or libel of any kind. We seek to be humble intellects here on the forums as it is the core of our mission. We take any attempt to undermine that mission very seriously.
